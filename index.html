The Opportunity

Global raw silk market value exceeds US$20 billion, growing ~7% annually. Kenya's climate and cost-effective workforce positions us perfectly to capture this opportunity while creating sustainable impact.

  • Ideal climate conditions for year-round production
  • Strong agricultural workforce
  • Growing global demand for sustainable silk

To pioneer Africa's leading silk production platform, empowering 100,000 farmers while establishing Kenya as a global leader in sustainable silk production.

  • 4x increase in farmer livelihoods
