Understanding Sericiulture

Rearing Steps

Sericulture involves producing silk by rearing silkworms for ~30 days, from egg to cocoon harvesting for silk filament. Silk rearing involves three key steps:

  • Hatching high quality silkworm eggs
  • Feeding worms quality mulberry leaves. They go though 5 instars (5 growth Stages)
  • Coccooning where worms filaments to create a protective cocoon around it>
  • The filaments are spinned or reeled to make fabrics>
